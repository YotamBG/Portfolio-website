About me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I am a passionate programmer with a keen interest in multiple fields, constantly seeking opportunities to create something new, solve problems and automate processes in efficient ways.

There's nothing more satisfying for me than putting in the hard work on a programming project and witnessing the creation of something that didn't exist before. It's this drive that led me to specialize as a full-stack engineer, as it allows me to see the bigger picture of the projects I work on and improve every aspect.



As a full-stack developer, I prioritize using descriptive names for variables and ensuring that each class and function serves a clear purpose. I believe in the power of deleting unnecessary code to keep projects streamlined and maintain readability over cleverness. Consistency in coding style is important to me, and I strive to master the idioms of the languages I work with.

Moreover, I understand the significance of choosing the right architecture for each project, as it plays a crucial role in its success. Embracing the concept that code can always be improved, I engage in an endless and efficient cycle of optimization. I strongly believe that software development is a team sport, and I value collaboration as a means to achieve outstanding results.

Outside of programming, I am deeply curious about various fields, drawing inspiration from dance and language learning. These passions have taught me valuable skills, including discipline, creativity, and adaptability, which I readily apply to my work as a developer. Additionally, my fluency in English, Hebrew, and Spanish enables effective communication and collaboration with diverse teams and stakeholders.

I am well-versed in using Git for version control, and I recognize the importance of automated tests to ensure software stability and reliability. With a strong work ethic, I approach every project with dedication and a commitment to delivering high-quality results.

As I embark on my career, I am seeking a position that offers continuous growth and learning opportunities. Starting as a junior developer, I am eager to contribute my skills and expertise while progressing towards a senior role. I am looking for a supportive and nurturing environment that values personal and professional development. With my potential, enthusiasm, and dedication to growth, I am confident in my ability to make valuable contributions to a team that encourages innovation and fosters continuous improvement.
